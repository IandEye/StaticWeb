Project Arogya


Better health is central to human happiness and well-being. It also makes an important contribution to economic progress, as healthy populations live longer, are more productive, and save more. In a role as a visually challenged student, it is important that they are both physically and emotionally healthy. You can't do a good job in school, if you don't feel well. Being healthy makes you feel good and allow you to perform more effectively. You can maintain your health by taking care of yourself and avoiding toxic situations. This Project encompasses conducting periodical eye, dental and general health checkups to the visually challenged. Organizing eye surgeries based on the eye checkups and feedback from the ophthalmologists. We also organize eye checkup camps at communities and Govt. schools to prevent eye disease, early detection of eye disease will reduce the visually challenged count. Hygiene Kit

Good personal hygiene is one of the most effective ways to prevent the development and spread of infections. Maintaining good personal hygiene and keeping the living environment and clothes clean and neat at all times improves living conditions for students.

Following are the targets for Hygiene Kit distribution:

  • Identify current knowledge, attitudes and practices related to menstruation
  • To increase awareness on menstrual hygiene among girls. Proper awareness will help the students be more confident and informed to manage menstrual hygiene.
  • Practicing proper hygiene and using sanitary materials can reduce individual risk of infection
  • Self-care and dignity for adolescent girls
  • Help the students to lead a Healthy and quality life.

Yoga

Making the students to inculcate the habit if Yoga into their daily routine is one of the aims of imparting Yoga training.
