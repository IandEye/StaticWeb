Education Model
Braille Classes

Even with advanced text-to-speech communications, there's simply nothing can compare to Braille for Visually challenged. Scrolling over the dots with finger, one can determine how words and sentences are structured. It is an invaluable tool for Visually challenged.

  • Provide Basic Braille classes to the Children so that they can lead their lives with an ease.
  • To teach the fundamentals of sentence structure, punctuation, etc.
  • Braille in Public Areas: ATM machines, bus stops, airports, parks, Offices, lifts and commercial places
  • Faculty, study material is provided for the students in addition to their regular academics at the school level to master Braille.
Integrated Education
  • Through local education, supported by well-prepared specialists, our children may enjoy everyday common experiences essential to the development of a keen awareness of the realities of the world around them. With proper technical assistance, consultation given to students, and a broad educational environment, our children are able to show their true worth; they are then more readily accepted socially by their sighted counterparts.
  • Statistics reveal that not even 10% of visually challenged children in most of the developing countries are receiving any kind of education, and therefore, integrated education is considered to be the only practical approach. It is the economically viable, psychologically superior, and socially acceptable model to bring all those unreached visually challenged children into the mainstream of education
  • We cnourage and join the outgoing School students in Intermediate in regular colleges along with sighted students.
  • For all graduation and post-graduation courses we will join the students in regular colleges along with the with sighted Students.
  • Tie-up with Intermediate and Degree Colleges to admit the Visually challenged students and put them up with sighted students. IandEye provides required study material, Laptops, MP3 players / recorders to the students so that they are equipped to compete with normal students. The students who have the potential to aim higher and crack the Public service commission exams, are joined in IAS academy. IandEye has the tie-up with such Academy as well. During the course of their academics, the students are required to take up additional optional courses for better job prospects. For any such situations, the Students are encouraged to take them up and the required fee is paid by IandEye other than their regular academic fee. All the payments related to exams, optional trainings, additional books and courses and any other study related material is taken care by IandEye.

Communication Skills

All schooling will be in local language, communication skill is very essential for our students to adopt integrated education model. It also helps in presenting one’s ideas and thoughts, improve ability to question, listen, form a rapport to make the Students feel more confident when dealing with real-life situations and in Professional Environments. Training is provided to develop interpersonal skills and communicate with Classmates, Teachers, Volunteers and in Professional environments. The training starts from the School level and is extended to students till their Graduation. The perceived output of these trainings are:

  • Improved Communication Skills
  • More expressive and Vocal
  • Develop Self-confidence
